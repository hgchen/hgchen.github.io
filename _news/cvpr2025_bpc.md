The **CIRP Lab** co-hosted the **CVPR 2025 Bin-Picking Workshop and Competition** with **OpenCV** and **Intrinsic**.

The event brought together over **450 teams** worldwide to tackle **real-world industrial robotic perception** challenges, advancing vision-based bin-picking through simulation-to-reality innovation and robust scene understanding.
A total of **$60,000 in prizes** were awarded to top-performing teams.

Learn more at [bpc.opencv.org](https://bpc.opencv.org/) and explore the open-source codebase at [github.com/opencv/bpc](https://github.com/opencv/bpc).
