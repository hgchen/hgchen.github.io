Grateful to receive a [$59,152 grant from the Hawaiʻi Community Foundation (Ingeborg v.F. McKee Fund)](https://www.hawaiicommunityfoundation.org/)
to develop computer vision and AI methods for early screening of mild cognitive decline and Alzheimer’s disease.
This project aims to advance digital health diagnostics in Hawaiʻi and beyond.
